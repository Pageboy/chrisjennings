Chris Jennings - Self Portrait
Last updated:
Wed, Mar 20, 2002
This was taken in the garden of a neighbour. We made this 'bus' out of the dirt. One of the those hot summer days before suncream was essential! I can remember most of these people. Forgive me if I get this wrong: Frome the left: Sandra Washington, Me, Robert Wright, Alex Urguart, Alan Hamp, Steve Washington, Paul Wright, Peter Hamp. date: probably 1961.
All I did to this image was to make a duplicate layer and then slightly modify the colour of that layer and then erased myself away back to the yellowish layer. This started as a black and white print and I needed to repair a fold in the print (I hope you can't see it!).
