Chris Jennings - Self Portrait Last updated: Wed, Mar 20, 2002
I had this Volkswagen Camper van with Sarah. We did a lot of excursions to Wales and Scotland. It had cooking facilities and lots of cupboards. I painted the bumpers bright yellow. I wish I had kept it!
The engine siezed up on the M40 and I had a reconditioned engine fitted. It was never the same and constantly broke down. I think this picture was taken by the River Usk in Wales.
