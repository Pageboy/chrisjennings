Chris Jennings - Self Portrait Last updated: 3/20/02
I converted this ex Rentokil landrover for camping. It was very slow and smelly because it ran on Diesel fuel. I did a lot of repairs on the engine and even rebored the block! In the days before power steering, driving this beast was exausting!
We went to some fairly remote places and got well off the road. Here in Perthshire, Sarah is holding a page from the Sunday Times with one of my megalith pictures.
