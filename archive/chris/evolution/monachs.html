Chris Jennings - Self Portrait Last updated: Wed, Mar 20, 2002
Summer times in Ardpatrick meant crewing for the 'Deli', trips around the Scottish west coast. On a voyage out to St. Kilda we visited the Monach Isles and this abandoned lighthouse was irresistable to Fran and me.
We took pictures of each other with the rain blowing in horizontally. When I look at this picture, I realise, how risky it was! The iron balcony was probably 100 years old
